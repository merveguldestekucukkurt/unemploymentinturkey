Qualified Education

QUALIFIED EDUCATION

Agenda of all citizens of the increasing unemployment figures in Turkey constitutes the first substance. While a road map on the subject has not been announced at the economic evaluation meetings held in the first month of 2020, the unemployed people under the age of 35 came together and established the 'Youth Unemployed Platform'.

In order to solve unemployment in Africa, efforts have been made to increase the quality of education. To increase the employability of young people by investing both in the quality and quality of education and by offering more opportunities to vocational education. This was the most basic strategy in Africa. Studies have been organized by the State to improve the quality of education in Africa.

In order to solve the unemployment problem in our country, our current education system needs to be revised in a way that can bring professional qualifications and competencies that can be valid anywhere in the world. For example, I will be a mathematics teacher, but I do not have the necessary academic qualifications to teach in America. I just have job opportunities in turkey. Unfortunately, this situation constitutes the young unemployed. Turkey Internet, social media and mobile user statistics, 62 million Internet users, 74% of Turkey's population.54 million social media users, 64% of Turkey's population.77 million mobile users, Turkey constitute 92% of the population. We can see that too many people use social media by looking at these statistics.

Therefore, we can open a social media account to increase the quality of education. In this account, we can publish the video of people who have graduated from university and cannot find a job. We can also produce content about how our education system is in this account and what needs to be changed. We can prepare posters stating young unemployed people and place them on the boards of the schools. In this way, we can increase the followers of the social media account.

This is how I adapted this solution in Africa to our country. Thanks to this solution, young unemployed people can hear their voices. Also, there is no need to have a strong material (money) to realize the solution. Only a large number of followers is needed to realize a solution.
