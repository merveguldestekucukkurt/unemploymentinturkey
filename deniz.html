Population Planning

POPULATION PLANNING

The development of the population will be checked as the family planning organizations so as to solve the unemployment issue. Due to the unplanned family members increased more people are being unemployed due to the limited employment opportunities. Also since only one family member of a family is financing the whole family's expenses his or her job may not be sufficient for them. Therefore, family arranging projects will be implemented widely and effectively.

How will that be done by organizations in Turkey?

First step is rehabilitate people's awareness rate about sexual health, mother-child health and reproductive health by organizing large groups of people with a consultancy in a public place like a state schools classroom during weekends. Some people think that they should make a lot of babies because of their culture and they do not think of the long term disadvantages of having four or more children. Therefore rehabilitating people especially from the east region will highly be beneficial for family planing.

Second one is, young population will be trained by the instructors for understanding their sexuality in schools, and in order to protect themselves from sexual illness and most important, unintentional pregnancy. 30% of the total populace is younger than eighteen, as indicated by UNEP, which is the reason teaching kids and youthful grown-ups about natural issues is significant to long haul achievement. This will assist them with cultivating an awareness of others expectations and "proactive citizenship," so when they become grown-ups they will settle on decisions that help their society as opposed to give damage to it.

In some part of our country, in urban regions, the utilization of print, communication, and Internet media can be an incredible method to build training and mindfulness. Therefore as the third step, volunteer educators must teach those people about financial problems of a big family due to the increase in family members.
